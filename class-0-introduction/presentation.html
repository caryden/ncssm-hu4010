About Bill Aulet

Bill Aulet is the Managing Director of the Martin Trust Center for MIT Entrepreneurship and Professor of the Practice at the MIT Sloan School of Management. He has been instrumental in developing MIT's entrepreneurship curriculum and has helped launch hundreds of companies.

Before joining MIT, he had a 25-year career as a successful entrepreneur and executive, including 11 years at IBM.

SME vs IDE Entrepreneurship

SME

Small & Medium Enterprise

  • Local/regional focus
  • Linear growth
  • Lifestyle or family business
  • Examples: Restaurant, consulting firm

IDE

Innovation-Driven Enterprise

  • Global ambition
  • Exponential growth potential
  • Technology or innovation-based
  • Examples: Dropbox, Airbnb

This course focuses on IDE entrepreneurship, but the principles apply to both.
