### Pricing Section (Slides 24-32)

#### Slide 24: Section Divider
- **Type:** `slide-statement`
- **Content:**
  - Statement: "Step 16: Set Your Pricing Framework"
- **Visual:** DE wheel with Step 16 glowing

#### Slide 25: The Big Mistake
- **Type:** `slide-visual`
- **Content:**
  - Title: "How Most Entrepreneurs Price"
- **Visual:** Formula shown and crossed out:
  - "Price = My Costs + Some Margin"
  - Big red X over it
- **Animation:** Formula appears, then gets rejected
- **Point:** "This leaves money on the table"

#### Slide 26: The Right Approach
- **Type:** `slide-visual`
- **Content:**
  - Title: "Value-Based Pricing"
- **Visual:** Correct formula:
  - "Price = Customer Value × 10-20%"
- **Animation:** Formula reveals with emphasis
- **Color:** Success green highlight

#### Slide 27: Value-Based Example
- **Type:** `slide-visual`
- **Content:**
  - Title: "Example Calculation"
- **Visual:** Step-by-step calculation:
  - Your product saves 10 hours/week
  - Customer's time worth $50/hour
  - Weekly value: 10 × $50 = $500
  - Annual value: $500 × 52 = $26,000
  - Price at 15%: $3,900/year (~$325/month)
- **Animation:** Each step calculates
- **Emphasis:** "That's your price ceiling"

#### Slide 28: Three Pricing Approaches
- **Type:** `slide-comparison`
- **Content:**
  - Three columns:
    1. **Cost-Plus**: Calculate costs + margin
       - Simple but suboptimal
    2. **Competitive**: Match/undercut others
       - Safe but commoditizes
    3. **Value-Based**: Price to value
       - Captures most value (recommended)
- **Animation:** Columns appear, third highlights
- **Color:** Third column emphasized

#### Slide 29: Pricing Psychology
- **Type:** `slide-visual`
- **Content:**
  - Title: "Pricing Tactics That Work"
- **Visual:** Grid of tactics:
  - Anchoring: Show high price first
  - Tiers: Good/Better/Best (most buy middle)
  - Charm pricing: $9.99 vs $10
  - Round numbers: $100 feels premium
- **Animation:** Each tactic with visual example
- **Interactive:** Show psychological effect

#### Slide 30: Pricing Tiers Example
- **Type:** `slide-visual`
- **Content:**
  - Title: "The Power of Three"
- **Visual:** Three pricing tiers:
  - Basic: $9/mo (limited features)

  - Enterprise: $99/mo (all features)
- **Animation:** Tiers appear, "Most Popular" badge on middle
