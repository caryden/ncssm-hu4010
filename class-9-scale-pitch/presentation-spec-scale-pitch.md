### Pitch Section (Slides 13-22)

#### Slide 13: Section Divider
- **Type:** `slide-statement`
- **Content:**
  - Statement: "Preparing Your Pitch"
- **Visual:** Presentation/microphone icon
- **Energy:** Transition to performance mode

#### Slide 14: 8 Minutes to Win
- **Type:** `slide-visual`
- **Content:**
  - Title: "Your Pitch Structure"
- **Visual:** Timeline showing 8 sections:
  1. Hook (30s)
  2. Problem (60s)
  3. Solution (60s)
  4. Market (60s)
  5. Business Model (60s)
  6. Traction (60s)
  7. Team (30s)
  8. Ask/Close (60s)
- **Animation:** Timeline builds with section markers
- **Color:** Each section distinct color

#### Slide 15: The Hook
- **Type:** `slide-two-part`
- **Content:**
  - Primary: "Hook (30 seconds)"
  - Secondary: "Grab attention immediately"
  - Options:
    - Provocative question
    - Surprising statistic
    - Brief story
- **Visual:** Hook icon (fishing hook metaphor)
- **Example:** "What if I told you half your homework time is wasted?"

#### Slide 16: Problem & Solution
- **Type:** `slide-comparison`
- **Content:**
  - Left (Problem - 60s):
    - Make it vivid and relatable
    - Use your persona by name
    - Quantify the pain
    - Why current solutions fail
  - Right (Solution - 60s):
    - One sentence what you do
    - How it works (simple)
    - Demo or visual if possible
    - Key differentiator
- **Animation:** Side by side

#### Slide 17: Market & Business Model
- **Type:** `slide-comparison`
- **Content:**
  - Left (Market - 60s):
    - TAM (with methodology)
    - Beachhead strategy
    - Expansion path
    - Why now?
  - Right (Business Model - 60s):
    - How you charge
    - Pricing
    - Unit economics (LTV/COCA)
- **Animation:** Side by side

#### Slide 18: Traction & Validation
- **Type:** `slide-two-part`
- **Content:**
  - Primary: "Traction (60 seconds)"
  - Secondary: "Prove it's not just an idea"
  - Show:
    - Customer conversations (quotes!)
    - Tests run and results
    - Early users or commitments
    - What you learned and how you pivoted
- **Visual:** Evidence/proof icon
- **Note:** "This is where the semester's work pays off"

#### Slide 19: Team & Ask
- **Type:** `slide-comparison`
- **Content:**
  - Left (Team - 30s):
    - Why you can execute
    - Relevant skills/experience
    - Brief, confident
  - Right (Ask & Close - 60s):
    - What you need next (specific!)
    - Clear next steps
    - Memorable closing
    - Return to your hook
- **Animation:** Final sections

#### Slide 20: Pro Tips
- **Type:** `slide-list`
- **Content:**
  - Title: "Pitch Like a Pro"
  - List:
    - Start strong—first 30 seconds matter most
    - Tell stories, not statistics
    - Show, don't just tell (demos > descriptions)
    - Use specific numbers ($500K, not "big market")
    - Practice transitions
    - Nail the close
- **Animation:** Each tip with icon
- **Visual:** Checkmarks as tips

#### Slide 21: All Team Members Present
- **Type:** `slide-statement`
- **Content:**
  - Statement: "Every team member must speak."
- **Subtext:** "Divide sections. Practice handoffs."
- **Animation:** Emphasis on requirement
- **Note:** Non-negotiable

#### Slide 22: Preparing for Q&A
- **Type:** `slide-list`
- **Content:**
  - Title: "Anticipate Tough Questions"
  - List:
    - "What if a competitor copies you?"
    - "How do you know customers will pay?"
    - "Why can YOU do this?"
    - "What's your biggest risk?"
- **Technique:** "Listen fully. It's okay to say 'We don't know yet, but...'"
