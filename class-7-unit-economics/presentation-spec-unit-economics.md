### LTV Section (Slides 7-16)

#### Slide 7: Section Divider
- **Type:** `slide-statement`
- **Content:**
  - Statement: "Step 17: Calculate Lifetime Value (LTV)"
- **Visual:** DE wheel with Step 17 glowing

#### Slide 8: What is LTV?
- **Type:** `slide-two-part`
- **Content:**
  - Primary: "Customer Lifetime Value"
  - Secondary: "The total profit from an average customer over your entire relationship"
- **Visual:** Customer icon with dollar trail extending over time
- **Animation:** Time extension visualization

#### Slide 9: Why LTV Matters
- **Type:** `slide-list`
- **Content:**
  - Title: "LTV Tells You..."
  - List:
    - How much you can afford to spend acquiring customers
    - Whether customers are valuable enough to sustain a business
    - Why retention is so important
- **Animation:** Each point appears
- **Icon:** Dollar/trend icons

#### Slide 10: The LTV Formula
- **Type:** `slide-visual`
- **Content:**
  - Title: "The Formula"
- **Visual:** Formula display:
  ```
  LTV = (ARPU × Gross Margin) × Lifespan
  ```
- **Animation:** Formula appears with each component highlighting
- **Color:** Each variable has different accent color

#### Slide 11: Component 1 - ARPU
- **Type:** `slide-two-part`
- **Content:**
  - Primary: "ARPU: Average Revenue Per User"
  - Secondary: "What does the average customer pay per month/year?"
  - Includes:
    - Base subscription/purchase
    - Upsells and add-ons
    - Expansion revenue
- **Visual:** Revenue components stacking
- **Note:** "Use realistic averages"

#### Slide 12: Component 2 - Gross Margin
- **Type:** `slide-two-part`
- **Content:**
  - Primary: "Gross Margin"
  - Secondary: "Revenue minus direct costs to serve"
  - Includes:
    - Hosting/infrastructure
    - Customer support
    - Payment processing
    - COGS (physical goods)
- **Visual:** Revenue bar with costs subtracted
- **Typical:** "60-80% for SaaS, lower for physical"

#### Slide 13: Component 3 - Lifespan
- **Type:** `slide-visual`
- **Content:**
  - Title: "Customer Lifespan"
- **Visual:** Timeline showing customer journey
  - Start: Customer acquired
  - Middle: Months of usage
  - End: Customer churns
- **Formula:** "Lifespan = 1 / Monthly Churn Rate"
- **Example:** "5% monthly churn = 20 month lifespan"

#### Slide 14: The Power of Retention
- **Type:** `slide-visual`
- **Content:**
  - Title: "Small Churn Changes = Huge LTV Changes"
- **Visual:** Animated comparison table:
  | Churn | Lifespan | LTV |
  |-------|----------|-----|
  | 10%/mo | 10 mo | $350 |
  | 5%/mo | 20 mo | $700 |
  | 2%/mo | 50 mo | $1,750 |
  (at $50 ARPU, 70% margin)
- **Animation:** Bars grow dramatically different heights
- **Emphasis:** "Retention is everything"

#### Slide 15: LTV Calculation Example
- **Type:** `slide-visual`
- **Content:**
  - Title: "Example Calculation"
- **Visual:** Step-by-step:
  - ARPU: $50/month
  - Gross Margin: 70%
  - Monthly Churn: 5% → Lifespan: 20 months
  - LTV = $50 × 0.70 × 20 = **$700**
- **Animation:** Each step calculates
- **Color:** Final result in accent color

#### Slide 16: Workshop Transition
- **Type:** `slide-statement`
- **Content:**
  - Statement: "Calculate YOUR LTV"
- **Subtext:** "15 minutes: ARPU, Margin, Lifespan, LTV"

#### Slide 19: Funnel Math
- **Type:** `slide-visual`
- **Content:**

- **Visual:** Calculation:
  - Goal: 20 customers/month
  - Close rate: 6% of leads
  - Leads needed: 333/month
- **Animation:** Math works backward up the funnel
- **Key insight:** "This tells you what marketing must deliver"

#### Slide 20: What is COCA?
- **Type:** `slide-two-part`
- **Content:**
  - Primary: "COCA: Cost of Customer Acquisition"
  - Secondary: "Total cost to acquire one new paying customer"
- **Visual:** Dollar going in, customer coming out
- **Formula:**
  ```
  COCA = (Marketing + Sales Costs) / Customers Acquired
  ```

#### Slide 21: COCA Components
- **Type:** `slide-visual`
- **Content:**
  - Title: "What Goes Into COCA?"
- **Visual:** Two buckets:
  - Marketing: Ads, content, events, tools, salaries
  - Sales: Salaries, commissions, travel, tools
- **Animation:** Items drop into buckets
- **Warning:** "Don't forget founder time!"

#### Slide 22: Founder Time Matters
- **Type:** `slide-two-part`
- **Content:**
  - Primary: "Include Your Time"
  - Secondary: "If you spend 20 hours/week selling, that has a cost"
- **Calculation:**
  - 20 hrs × $100/hr (opportunity cost) = $2,000/week
  - $8,000/month in founder sales cost
- **Animation:** Time → money visualization
- **Note:** "Most founders undercount this"

#### Slide 23: COCA Calculation Example
- **Type:** `slide-visual`
- **Content:**
  - Title: "Example Calculation"
- **Visual:** Cost breakdown:
  | Category | Monthly Cost |
  |----------|-------------|
  | Paid ads | $3,000 |
  | Content | $1,000 |
  | Founder time | $4,000 |
  | Tools | $500 |
  | **Total** | **$8,500** |

  Customers: 10/month
  **COCA = $850**
- **Animation:** Costs add up, division calculates

#### Slide 24: Workshop Transition

  - Statement: "Calculate YOUR COCA"
- **Subtext:** "10 minutes: All costs, customer projection, COCA"

## Key Visualizations to Build

### 1. LTV Churn Comparison (Slide 14)
```
Structure:
- Three horizontal bars representing LTV at different churn rates
- Labels: Churn %, Lifespan, LTV value
- Dramatic size differences

Animation:
1. First bar (10% churn) grows to $350
2. Second bar (5% churn) grows 2x longer to $700
3. Third bar (2% churn) grows 5x longer to $1,750
4. "Retention is everything" label appears

Technical:
- D3 bar chart with animated width
- Staggered timing for dramatic effect
- Value labels update as bars grow
```

### 2. Sales Funnel with Math (Slide 18-19)
```
Structure:
- Tapered funnel shape
- Stage labels with conversion rates
- Running totals at each stage

Animation:
1. Funnel outline draws
2. Top stage fills (100 leads)
3. Each subsequent stage fills with fewer
4. Conversion math appears beside each stage
5. Bottom shows final customers

Technical:
- SVG funnel with variable widths
- Number counters at each stage
- Conversion percentage labels
```

### 3. COCA Calculator (Slide 23)
```
Structure:
- Table of costs
- Running total
- Division by customers
- Final COCA result

Animation:
1. Cost categories appear row by row
2. Amounts fill in
3. Total sums up
4. "÷ 10 customers" appears
5. COCA result calculates with emphasis

Technical:
- D3 table with animated rows
- Counter animations for numbers
- Final result highlight/glow
```

### 4. Ratio Gauge (Slide 26)
```
Structure:
- Semi-circular gauge/speedometer
- Colored zones (red → green)
- Needle/indicator
- Zone labels

Animation:
1. Gauge outline appears
2. Color zones fill in
3. Labels appear (Crisis, Danger, etc.)
4. Needle starts at 0
5. Needle animates to position (interactive?)

Technical:
- SVG arc segments for zones
- Animated needle rotation
- Color gradients for zones
```

### 5. Improvement Levers (Slide 27)
```
Structure:
- Two columns: Increase LTV, Decrease COCA
- Lever icons for each action
- Connection to ratio improvement

Animation:
1. Central ratio display
2. LTV column appears with levers
3. COCA column appears with levers
4. Pulling lever → ratio improves (visual demo)

Technical:
- CSS grid layout
- Icon animations for levers
- Ratio counter updates
```
